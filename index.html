 Who are we? 

The Science Club is the place for students who are interested in science to learn about all scopes of science from physics, chemistry, maths & different disciplines of engineering to the environment. We discuss how to use this conceptual basis for applications in the real world. Trips will be taken to museums, observatories, research institutes and so on.Our club aims to inculcate the spirit of science into students in daily life. We wish to nurture the habit of implementing book knowledge into practicality. We will focus on long term, academically-focused relationships between youth club members and practicing scientists. Our club will also work to serve to society especially problems related to various industries (like Water recycling in plants) and Common man for senior citizen. We want more students to get registered in IEEE , VIPNET and WIE. We are now planning to open Computer Society now. The aims of this club will be fulfilled by organizing various activities such as:- 1. Gurukul Shiksha Sopan 2. Fun with Science and Technical activities 3. Expert's lecture from professors of IIT's. NIT's and Universities 4. Project Exhibition 5.Students's National Journal 6. Blog, Article, Chapter and Book writing 7. Summer camps 8. Project submission 9. International and National level Quiz Competitions













 What we stand for? 

Our Vision

To be the flagship into implementing Science to problems around us and set new standards into innovations.

Our Mission

To include and involve students to excel into innovation and be the solution providers of tomorrow by doing exemplary works into research and innovations.
